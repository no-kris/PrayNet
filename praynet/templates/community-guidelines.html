Community Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Spam

Avoid posting spammy content or engaging in excessive self-promotion.

3. Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to the community's purpose. Off-topic posts may be removed.

4. Protect Privacy

Do not share personal information about others without consent.

5. Report Issues

If you encounter any violations of these guidelines, please report them at wepray.support@gmail.com.
